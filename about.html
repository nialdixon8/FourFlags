Four Flags

After an evening of playing some basketball, we wanted to see if there were any casual basketball tournaments in the local area, to our surprise there was absolutely nothing! Despite being disappointed we thought of a great idea to just organise our own events so that nobody would have to endure the same disappointment we went through.

Whilst we have currently provided several basketball events to the Worcester area, we want to quickly provide this same focus to the rest of the UK.

When organising our events, we focus on developing a community of teams and players, and whilst our tournaments are competitive, we encourage all skill levels to take part. During tournaments you can compete for the title, chat with the other players, and sit and chill whilst watching the other games.
